About our Firm :

Kathmandu University Computational Mathematics Club is a departmental club under Department of Mathematics in Kathmandu University, Nepal.

Our Club's History
Motive

2018 - KUCMC :

We are Known as KUCMC and are working for the welfare of students enrolled in the program "B.Sc. in Computational Mathematics" here at Kathmandu University.

Our Club's History

Established in 2018,

Recognizing the need for a dedicated space within our department, a group of enthusiastic and visionary students came together to form KUCMC, in 2018. Through their collective efforts, they worked diligently to gain recognition from the university administration and garner support from faculty members. With their determination and perseverance, KUCMC officially became a recognized club, opening doors for students to engage in various academic and extracurricular activities. Since its inception, KUCMC has grown exponentially, attracting students from diverse backgrounds who share a common passion for their respective disciplines.

Every year, KUCMC holds elections to select a new board that will lead and guide the club forward.Leading up to the elections, interested individuals are given the opportunity to nominate themselves for various positions on the board. These positions typically include President, Vice President,General Secretary,club secretary Treasurer, and other executive members as determined by the club's constitution. Once nominations are received, candidates have the opportunity to present their visions, plans, and qualifications to the members. This democratic process allows students to actively participate in shaping the future of the club and ensures a smooth transition of leadership.

In addition to holding annual elections for the new board, KUCMC also conducts interviews as part of its selection process for certain positions within the club.

Our Philosophy:

KUCMC (Kathmandu University Departmental Club) was established in 2018 with the aim of fostering a sense of unity and creating a platform for students in Nepal to explore their academic interests and passions..Through a wide range of events, workshops, and seminars, KUCMC has played a pivotal role in nurturing the academic and personal growth of its members. Today, KUCMC stands as a testament to the vision and dedication of its founding members and continues to make a significant impact on the student community at Kathmandu University in Nepal.
